## 6. Permissions Requested by the App

To provide its core functionality (like reminders and notifications), Zyphen requests the following device permissions. We only request permissions that are strictly necessary for the app to function:

* **Notifications** (`POST_NOTIFICATIONS`): Required to show you timely reminders and alerts from the app.
* **Alarms & Reminders** (`SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM`): Required to ensure that your scheduled reminders fire exactly when you need them.
* **Run at Startup** (`RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED`): Required to reschedule your reminders and alarms silently in the background after you restart your device so you don't miss them.

These permissions do not grant us access to your personal files, contacts, or location, and all reminder data remains stored locally on your device.
